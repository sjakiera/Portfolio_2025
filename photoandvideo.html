Photography

Since my early years, I've been captivated by the art of videography and photography. I find joy in exploring diverse skills to create compelling content, whether for commercial purposes or artistic expression. Experimenting with different techniques is my forte, and my heart lies in the world of analog. Shooting with classic cameras, analog photography and filmmaking are my preferred avenues for capturing moments in a distinctive and timeless manner.
