About me

I’m Shakira Bouti, a 23-year-old based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, currently studying Communications and Multimedia at Hogeschool van Amsterdam. I specialize in UX/UI design, but I consider myself an all-round digital designer with a curiosity for all things creative and technological.

I have a wide range of interests and enjoy diving into new developments, whether it’s experimenting with the latest design tools, exploring AI, or learning about human behavior and emerging digital trends. My creativity doesn’t stop at academics, I actively work on personal projects, from organizing photo shoots to mastering new software, which allows me to combine theory with hands-on experience.

Adaptability is one of my strengths: I can move seamlessly between team collaboration and independent work, always approaching projects with dedication and a focus on user-centered solutions.

In short, I’m a dynamic and curious designer who loves exploring new ideas, pushing the boundaries of design and technology, and creating experiences that leave a meaningful impact on users.
